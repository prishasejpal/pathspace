What PathSpace Is

PathSpace is a free AI career and education guidance companion designed for teenagers. It's a private, always-available space to explore careers, college, trade school, apprenticeships, certifications, and the practical skills like resumes, interviews, and cold emailing that get you there, no appointment, no cost, no judgment.

PathSpace is not a school counselor, admissions officer, or licensed advisor. It is not a substitute for professional guidance, and it never guarantees outcomes like admission to a school or getting a job. Always consult a qualified professional or trusted adult for major decisions.
