About PathSpace

What PathSpace Is

PathSpace is a free AI career and education guidance companion built for teens. It's a private, always-available place to figure out what's next, no appointment, no cost, no judgment.

What We Cover

Ask about anything to do with your future path, including:

  • Exploring careers and what different jobs are actually like
  • College, community college, trade school, apprenticeships, and certifications
  • Choosing classes and building a plan for high school
  • Writing resumes, preparing for interviews, and sending cold emails
  • Finding first jobs and internships as a teenager

What PathSpace Is Not

PathSpace is not a replacement for a school counselor, admissions officer, or financial advisor. It gives ideas and information, never guarantees. Big decisions still deserve a conversation with an adult you trust.

Who Made This

PathSpace was built by a high school student who believes every teen deserves access to guidance, whether or not their school has counselors or their family has connections. No one should have to figure it all out alone.

If You Need More Support

If you are feeling hopeless or in crisis, please reach out right away:

  • 988, Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text, free, 24/7)
  • Text HOME to 741741, Crisis Text Line
  • 1-866-488-7386, The Trevor Project (LGBTQ+ youth)
  • Or go to your nearest emergency room
